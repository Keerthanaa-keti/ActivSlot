ActivSlot - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Introduction

ActivSlot ("we", "our", or "the app") is a walking and fitness planner for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Health Data

With your permission, ActivSlot reads step counts, active energy, and workout data from Apple HealthKit. This data is used solely to display your activity progress, suggest walk times, and track streaks. We never write to HealthKit.

Calendar Data

With your permission, ActivSlot reads your calendar events to find free time slots for walking. Calendar data is used only to generate walk suggestions. We never modify or delete your calendar events without your explicit action.

Preferences

Settings you configure (step goals, preferred walk times, notification preferences) are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your daily step count and activity progress
  • Suggest optimal walking times based on your calendar availability
  • Track walking streaks and goal completion
  • Send local notifications for walk reminders (if enabled)

Data Storage and Security

All personal data (health data, calendar data, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your health or calendar data to any external server. Subscription status is managed through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit: Used to read health and fitness data. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used to manage in-app subscriptions. Purchase transactions are handled by Apple.
  • Microsoft Identity (optional): If you choose to connect an Outlook calendar, authentication is handled by Microsoft's sign-in service. We only access calendar event times and titles.

Your Rights

  • You can revoke HealthKit or Calendar permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete all local app data by deleting the app.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

ActivSlot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
